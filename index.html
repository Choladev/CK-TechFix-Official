About WAHDA

WAHDA Community School is a beacon of hope and education in Ng'ombe, Lusaka, Zambia. We are dedicated to providing quality education to children from diverse backgrounds in a nurturing and inclusive environment.

Founded in 2005, our school has grown from a small community initiative to a respected educational institution serving over 300 students annually.

Our vision is to empower the next generation through knowledge, discipline, and strong moral values, creating future leaders who will make a positive impact in their communities.
